Introduction to the PMI Liquid Permeameter

The PMI Liquid Permeameter pumps a liquid (typically water or oil) through one of two chambers at a predefined pressure and measures the volume of liquid that flows through the sample. The flow and pressure is used to calculate the permeability of the sample.

The temperatures of the chambers can be set from 0 to 200 degrees Celsius. The pressure can be set from 0 to 100 PSI. All is software controlled including custom pre-programmed profiles of temperature and pressure over time. The data is displayed in real time and a report is generated that displays the graphs of flow, temperature, pressure and permeability. Data is saved in a .pmi file that can be opened with any text editor. The data can also be exported to excel format.
